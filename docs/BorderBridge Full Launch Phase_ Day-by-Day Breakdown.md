## Full Launch Phase Success Criteria

The following criteria define success for the full launch phase:

1. **User Acquisition:**
   - Minimum 1,000 registered users by end of launch period
   - At least 100 paid subscriptions activated
   - Customer acquisition cost within target range
   - Acquisition growth trend positive day-over-day

2. **Platform Performance:**
   - 99.9% uptime throughout public launch
   - Average page load time under 2 seconds
   - All core features functioning without critical issues
   - Successful scaling to accommodate user growth

3. **User Satisfaction:**
   - Initial NPS score of 40+
   - Customer support satisfaction rating of 90%+
   - Feature satisfaction ratings averaging 4/5 or higher
   - Positive public reviews and testimonials

4. **Operational Efficiency:**
   - Support ticket resolution within SLA targets
   - Knowledge base covering 90%+ of common questions
   - All core processes documented and optimized
   - Team successfully transitioned to regular operations
