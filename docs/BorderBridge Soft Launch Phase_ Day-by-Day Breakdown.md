# BorderBridge Soft Launch Phase: Day-by-Day Breakdown

## Soft Launch Phase Overview (Days 11-20)

The soft launch phase introduces BorderBridge to a limited audience of beta users, allowing for real-world testing, feedback collection, and system optimization before the full public launch. This controlled release helps identify and address any issues while beginning to build momentum through initial marketing activities.

## Soft Launch Phase Success Criteria

Before proceeding to the Full Launch phase, the following criteria must be met:

1. **Technical Performance:**
   - Platform stability with 99.9% uptime during beta
   - All critical bugs identified during beta resolved
   - System performance validated under increased load
   - Payment processing successfully tested with real transactions

2. **User Validation:**
   - Minimum 70% of beta users actively engaged with platform
   - At least 10 positive testimonials collected
   - No major usability issues reported by multiple users
   - Successful completion of core user journeys by majority of testers

3. **Marketing Readiness:**
   - Launch marketing assets created and approved
   - PR outreach prepared with scheduled releases
   - Social media and email campaigns ready for activation
   - Initial case studies and success stories documented

4. **Operational Readiness:**
   - Support team fully trained and scaled for launch
   - Knowledge base comprehensive and updated with beta learnings
   - Subscription management and billing support processes tested
   - Launch day support plan finalized
