## Implementation Instructions

### For Digital Use

1. Convert each Markdown template to your preferred document format (DOCX, Google Docs, etc.)
2. Apply the color scheme, typography, and formatting as specified in the formatting guide
3. Replace placeholder text (indicated by [brackets]) with your specific content
4. Add your logo and any additional brand elements
5. Save as templates in your document management system

### For Print Use

1. Follow steps 1-4 above
2. Ensure adequate margins for printing (minimum 1 inch on all sides)
3. Test print to verify formatting and readability
4. Consider using slightly heavier paper stock (24-28 lb) for proposals and welcome guides

### Customization Tips

- Maintain consistent branding across all documents
- Update templates periodically to reflect service changes or brand evolution
- Consider creating variations for different client segments or service tiers
- Develop a version control system to track template updates
