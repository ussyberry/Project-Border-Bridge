# BorderBridge Financial Projections
## First 3 Months of Operations

## Executive Summary

This document presents comprehensive financial projections for BorderBridge's first three months of operations. BorderBridge is an AI-powered immigration guidance platform that offers personalized assessments, roadmaps, document preparation, and expert support through a tiered subscription model.

The projections show a classic early-stage SaaS trajectory with significant investment in growth driving short-term losses but rapidly improving unit economics. Key highlights include:

- **Revenue Growth**: From $690 in Month 1 to $7,222 in Month 3 (946% growth)
- **Customer Growth**: From 30 paid customers in Month 1 to 314 in Month 3 (947% growth)
- **Improving Unit Economics**: LTV:CAC ratio improving from 7.7:1 to 13.2:1
- **Cash Requirements**: Additional funding of approximately $100,000 needed by end of Month 3
- **Path to Profitability**: Projected break-even around Month 8-9 if current trends continue

These projections align with BorderBridge's 30-day launch plan and demonstrate a viable business model with strong unit economics, though additional funding will be required to maintain the growth trajectory beyond Month 3.
