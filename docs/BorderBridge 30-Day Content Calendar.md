The calendar is organized week by week, with specific content pieces scheduled for each day.

Each content piece is aligned with:
- A specific content pillar
- Target audience segment(s)
- Content format
- Distribution channel(s)
- Call-to-action

## Content Production Timeline

For each piece of content, the following production timeline should be followed:

- **2 Weeks Before:** Topic research and outline development
- **10 Days Before:** First draft completion
- **7 Days Before:** Review and revisions
- **5 Days Before:** Final approval
- **3 Days Before:** Asset creation (images, graphics, etc.)
- **2 Days Before:** Upload and scheduling
- **Publication Day:** Distribution and promotion
- **1 Day After:** Initial performance review
- **7 Days After:** Comprehensive performance analysis

## Performance Tracking

Each content piece should be tracked for:

1. **Engagement Metrics:**
   - Page views/video views
   - Time on page/view duration
   - Social shares
   - Comments and interactions
   - Email click-through rates

2. **Conversion Metrics:**
   - CTA click-through rate
   - Lead form completions
   - Free assessment signups
   - Trial conversions
   - Subscription attributions

3. **SEO Performance:**
   - Keyword rankings
   - Organic traffic
   - Backlinks generated
   - Featured snippets captured

## Content Optimization Process

1. **Weekly Performance Review:** Analyze top and bottom performing content
2. **A/B Testing:** Test headlines, formats, and CTAs for key content pieces
3. **Content Refreshes:** Update high-potential older content monthly
4. **Keyword Optimization:** Refine SEO strategy based on performance data
5. **Format Expansion:** Repurpose successful content into additional formats

## Calendar Flexibility Guidelines

This calendar serves as a structured framework but should remain flexible to accommodate:

1. **Breaking News:** Immigration policy changes requiring immediate coverage
2. **Performance-Based Adjustments:** Pivoting based on content performance data
3. **Seasonal Opportunities:** Adjusting for immigration program deadlines or seasonal trends
4. **User Feedback:** Addressing questions and topics requested by the community
5. **Strategic Business Needs:** Supporting product launches or business initiatives

The calendar should be reviewed weekly and adjusted as needed while maintaining the overall balance across content pillars and target segments.
